Phoenix

Phoenix's star power shines through his fighting style, igniting the battlefield with flash and flare. Whether he's got backup or not, he's rushing in to fight on his own terms.

Abilities:


Signature - Curveball:

Cast a curving flare that bursts into brilliant light after a brief delay, temporarily blinding all looking at it.


Hot Hands:

Throw a fireball that explodes on impact with the ground in an AoE that damages enemies and heals you.


Blaze:

Cast out a flame wall that blocks vision and damages anyone passing through it.


Ultimate - Run It Back:

Mark your current location. If you die during this ability's duration, or when this ability's duration expires, you'll be reborn at the marked location with full health.
