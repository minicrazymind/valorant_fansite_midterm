Brimstone

Brimstone's orbital arsenal ensures his squad always has the advantage. His ability to deliver utility precisely and safely make him the unmatched boots-on-the-ground commander.

Abilities:


Signature - Sky Smoke:

Use your map to call in orbital deployment smokescreens that obscure vision. Click to set the locations, and confirm to launch.


Incendiary:

Launch an incendiary grenade that deploys a damaging field of fire.


Stim Beacon:

Target a nearby location to call in a Stim Beacon, giving players near it Rapidfire.


Ultimate - Orbital Strike:

Use your map to target a location, launching a devastating orbital strike that pulses for high damage over several seconds.
