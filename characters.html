Meet the Agents

Agents are the characters that you'll be able to pick up in the world of Valorant. While they aren't restricted in which guns and armor they buy, they do each come with four abilites.


Agents each have one signature ability, two regular abilities, and an ultimate ability. These abilities are what make them unique to the other characters and introduce new and creative ways to play the game.


Agents gain one charge of their signature ability at the start of every round and can then opt to purchase charges for their two regular abilities.


Ultimate abilities requires multiple kills to charge and are powerful abilities that can turn the tide of a gunfight.
