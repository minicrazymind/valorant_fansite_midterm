Jett

Jett's agile and evasive fighting style lets her take risks no one else can. She runs circles around every skirmish, cutting enemies up before they knew what hit them.

Abilities:


Signature - Tailwind:

Immediately dash a short distance in the direction you're moving.


Cloudburst:

Throw out a cloud of fog that obscures vision on impact. Hold down the ability button to bend the cloud's in-flight trajectory.


Updraft:

After a brief wind up, propel yourself upwards.


Ultimate - Blade Storm:

Arm yourself with several deadly throwing knives that deal moderate damage and kill on headshots. Scoring a kill restores all daggers.
