Cypher

Cypher is a one-man surveillance network who keeps tabs on the enemy's every move. No secret is safe. No maneuver goes unseen. Cypher is always watching.

Abilities:


Signature - Spycam:

Place a remote camera. After placing, reactivate to view the video feed. Left click while in camera to fire a tracking dart.


Trapwire (Free):

Place a stealthed tripwire between two walls. Triggering enemies are restrained, dazed, and revealed for a short time.


Cyber Cage:

Toss out a remote activation trap. Reactivate to create a cage that slows enemies who pass through it.


Ultimate - Neural Theft:

Extract information from the corpse of an enemy, revealing the location of their living allies.
