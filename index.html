What is Valorant?

Valorant a 5v5 character-based tactical shooter where each character has their own unique set of abilities and weapons.


How you utilize your character and your knowledge the map matters, but gunplay is the main focus for this game.


Games are separated into multiple rounds where teams will try to either wipe each other out or complete the objective such as defusing or defending a bomb.


Each round, players will be able to buy abilities, armor, and weapons with money earned from kills, victories, and a loss streaks.
